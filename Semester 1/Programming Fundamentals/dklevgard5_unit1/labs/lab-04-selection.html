Scenario:

I can hardly wait for Friday because if my pay check is big enough I'm going to
buy a new guitar at The Music Centre on Saturday. I'll be disappointed if I
can't get it.

Pseudocode:
BEGIN
  wait for Friday
  IF paycheck total is big enough THEN
    wait for Saturday
    go to The Music Centre
    buy guitar
  ELSE
    cry
    stamp foot
  ENDIF
END
